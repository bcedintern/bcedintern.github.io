Campgrounds

Campgaw Mountain Reservation

200 Campgaw Road
Mahwah, NJ 07430
201.372.3500
co.bergen.nj.us/bcparks/CampgawMountain

Campgaw Mountain Reservation offers unique opportunities such as archery, disc golf, skiing, snowboarding and snow tubing in a wooded setting. Hiking is available along the marked trails in this 1,373-acre wooded park. Campsites are also available at Campgaw. Visit Campgaw Moutntain Reservations website for Camping regulations and Camper information.

Camp Glen Gray

200 Midvale Mountain Road
Mahwah, NJ 07430
201.327.7234
www.glengray.org

Camp Glen Gray is an overnight camping and hiking facility located on the border of Oakland and Mahwah. Nestled among thousands of acres of county and state park lands, there's never a lack of things to do and see!
