Welcome to Bergen County

Home to nearly 200 arts and history organizations, 9,000 acres of parkland, and a variety of shopping and entertainment destinations, Bergen County is a great place to explore on your next vacation. Whether you’re looking for an outdoor adventure, a unique shopping experience, or a chance to view American history and culture from a first-hand perspective, you will find exactly what you’re looking for in Bergen County.
