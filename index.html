Welcome to Bergen County

Home to nearly 200 arts and history organizations, 9,000 acres of parkland, and a variety of shopping and entertainment destinations, Bergen County is a great place to explore on your next vacation. Whether you’re looking for an outdoor adventure, a unique shopping experience, or a chance to view American history and culture from a first-hand perspective, you will find exactly what you’re looking for in Bergen County.

The Park West Tavern


Food: Upscale New American, much of it more formal than the setting.

Ambience: Busy tavern and family restaurant.

Value: In line with the ingredients, though only sometimes with the service. Appetizers $5 to $16, entrées $14 to $44.

Would be Good For: Fine-dining dinner in laid-back setting.

Recommended Dishes: Sautéed Maine diver scallops, crispy-skin Atlantic salmon, chocolate bar dessert, strawberry shortcake.

Hours: 11:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. daily; bar open until 12:30 a.m. daily.

Liquor/Wine: An extensive and thoughtfully selected full bar, beer and wine selection.

Reservations: Recommended, and available on opentable.com.

Accommodations for children: Menu

Dress: Smart Casual

Takeout: Yes
