Getting Here

As the grand conduit through New York City by way of the George Washington Bridge, Bergen County is only 1 mile from the largest city in the United States. Given this ideal location, Bergen County over the years has developed an intricate and sophisticated road and mass transit network which provides transportation linkages to the surrounding metropolitan region and around the County through numerous miles of roads, 3 passenger rail lines along with 30 train stations, 51 NJ Transit bus routes, Edgewater Ferry Landing with direct access to Midtown Manhattan, Teterboro Airport and the GW Bridge.
